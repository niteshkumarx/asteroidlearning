Sketchup

Google Sketchup is a 3D modeling computer program for a wide range of drawing applications such as architectural, interior design, civil and mechanical engineering, film, and video game design and available in a freeware version, SketchUp Make, and a paid version with additional functionality, SketchUp Pro.

SketchUp is synonymous with friendly and forgiving 3D modeling software, it doesn't sacrifice usability for the sake of functionality. Start by drawing lines and shapes. Push and pull surfaces to turn them into 3D forms. Stretch, copy, rotate and paint to make anything you like. If you want to be productive within a couple of hours, Google Sketchup is the right thing for you. The even friendly thing with Sketchup is its compatiblity with multiple platforms and API's. You can even create a 3D model to be exported in some other platform.
